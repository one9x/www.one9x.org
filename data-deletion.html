One9x org Data Deletion

Your privacy matters to us at One9x org. We are committed to protecting your data and providing you with clear and easy-to-understand information about how we collect, use, and share your data across our app portfolio.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request the deletion of your data from all One9x org apps, please send an email to datadeletion@one9x.org. In your email, please include the following information:

  • App usernames for each One9x org app, you want your data to be deleted
  • Specific types of data you want to delete, such as personal information, location data, or usage data, for each app

What Data Is Deleted or Kept?

When you request data deletion, we will delete the following data associated with your One9x org accounts:

  • Personal information, such as your name, email address, and phone number
  • Location data
  • Usage data

We may retain certain data for a limited period of time for legal or compliance purposes, such as:

  • Anonymous usage data for analytics and research purposes
  • Data necessary to prevent fraud or abuse

Additional Information

If you have any questions about data deletion, please contact us at contact@one9x.org.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing you with control over your data across all One9x org apps. Thank you for choosing our apps!
